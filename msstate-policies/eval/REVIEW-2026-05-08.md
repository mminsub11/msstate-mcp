## Verdict

**No weak passes spotted.** The 37 passing positive cases each:

- Cite the expected OP number explicitly ("According to OP X...")
- Quote verbatim with quotation marks for normative claims; do not paraphrase binding language
- Use the F4 `primaryEvidence` windows correctly — short matched-passages without losing grounding when bodies are partial
- Hedge appropriately when the policy text doesn't directly cover a sub-question
- Use cross-references between retrieved policies thoughtfully (this is also why the F2 BM25 floor over-rejected when set to 11.5 — the cross-ref path is load-bearing for several weak-keyword questions)

The single fail (tornado, expected `01.04`) is correctly graded as a fail — the model refused with a redirect to MSU Emergency Management, and the judge agreed the question wasn't answered from policy text. **This is the right behavior** for a corpus-boundary case (OP 01.04 doesn't contain tornado protocols; the actual CEMP lives at `emergency.msstate.edu` outside our corpus).
